Round 2

The second quarter started the same way the first quarter had with Belgrave South dominating the clearances. All of the backline players kept on trying their hardest to stem the tide. 

Highlights of our first half were 

Tigerlily's two fantastic smothers, 

Dusty's work in the first half was first rate.

We made some changes going into the third quarter which worked in our favour. 

Josh was in and under every contest throughout the third quarter getting the ball out to his teammates. We got on the scoreboard with Xavier kicking the first goal. The hard work continued throughout the quarter with indi's good ruck work. 

Final quarter started well for us with our midfield getting well in top and giving our forward line the opportunity to further our score. We missed a couple of chances at goal, with Xavier kicking his second goal of the game. The players fort the game out with high spirits and enthusiasm. 

We played an outstanding team in Belgrave South and we must learn from today's game.
