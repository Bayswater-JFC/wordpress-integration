Round 1

Vs Blackburn Red
 
Game 1 for the season 2022 was the complete opposite of last years round 1. There was no hail, no rain and no child deep puddles to contend with. 
We were greeted with blue skies and perfect conditions. 
Todays game was similar to the conditions. Picture perfect. 
The team were celebrating the start of the season with the 50 game milestone of 2 of its players. And that inspiration was plenty to see them dig deep from the very first bounce. 
With some quick ball movement and sensational teamwork the first quarter ended with Bayswater up 4 goals to 0. 
From there the game was fairly even until the final siren with the first quarter setting up a fantastic win. 
The perfect way to start the season. Great game Bayswater. 

Round 2

Away vs Montrose Red.
Game 2 - ANZAC Round.
Fantastic footy conditions greeted us for the round which honours our ANZACs.
With both teams coming into today off the back of a first round win, the game was played in great spirits by all from the very first bounce.
A low scoring , physically demanding first half saw both teams entering the half time break already physically drained.
The third quarter saw our opponents kick ahead with some quick unanswered goals.
Again in the final quarter, both teams played similar, physical styles of footy with Bayswater unable to close the gap from the third quarter.

A great game of football by both teams with our opponents sneaking away with a win.
Onward to next week…….

Round 3

Home vs Wantirna south

Heading into round 3 with 1 win & 1 loss and looking to push hard for a second season win.
For the third week in a row it’s blue skies and sunshine. Mother Nature treating us well.

With injury starting to creep into the team we head in with no interchange available and unfortunately meet an opponent with a full bench.
As the game progresses we suffer a few minor injuries to our players and combined with no interchange the play is dictated by our opponent.

With a couple of goals being kicked against us each quarter our opponent takes the victory with a one sided scorecard however it didn’t reflect the Bayswater efforts to keep their scoring low by some great teamwork and hardball gets.

With their heads high, they look ahead, towards round 4.

Round 4

Away vs Heathmont.

Conditions were fantastic for our match with the team over the creek. Still no rain to be seen for season 2022.

Noticeable change in the Bayswater team today - they seem hungry for a win. Focused , determined and in every contest.
Pushed one of the top teams in the division hard all day with both teams hitting the scoreboard about the same amount of times.
Unfortunately a couple of goals extra to the opponent saw them victorious.
Lots of noticeable improvements and unlucky the ball didn’t go through the big sticks a little more.
Go Baysie.

Round 5

Home vs Norwood

Guess what - it’s sunny again. I could get used to this.

Today we hosted Norwood in what quickly was revealed to be a 15 vs 15 match up.
Today was anticipated to be a close contest and that it was.
Trailing by 1 goal at the end of the first quarter, the team lifted to be up at half time.
The third quarter saw Norwood edge in front and Baysie couldn’t close the gap in the fourth losing by 7 points.
A hard day with lots of running to cover the gaps.
Unlucky, but we will be ready next time.

Special thanks to Lachie, Charlie & Phoenix from the U11 team for jumping in and helping out today.

Round 6

Vs   Fair Park.

Game 6 for the season sees us hosting Fair Park in fantastic footy conditions.
Slightly cold, no rain to be seen, and every reason to run to keep warm.

The team were celebrating yet another milestone 50th game and that was todays motivation to get the job done.
That, and the desire to get another win against our name to break a drought.

A very even first half saw the scores level at the main break, but inaccurate kicking meant we missed an opportunity to be a few goals up.
The second half was all Bayswater.
With the boys finding a lot of ball up forward, the goals started coming and our lead began to increase.
The forth quarter allowed us the build a little more giving us a well earned victory against a firey opponent.

Great to see the passion today.
Go Baysie.
