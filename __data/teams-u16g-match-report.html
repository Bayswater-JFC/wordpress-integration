Round 1

Today we played Boronia at their ground.

Unfortunately we are a little low on numbers and with a couple of girls unable to play so we only had 11 to take the field.

Boronia sent a few players over to even up the numbers.

We had a really good first quarter. Our effort level was high, our talk was good and we made a lot of effort to get in all the right spots to give us the best opportunity to drive the ball forward. We took a good lead at quarter time.

The second quarter was much more even. The effort level was still really good but we just weren’t picking up their players enough so something we would work on in the third quarter.

Unfortunately we lost 2 players at half time so we had to play 2 players short which was just too hard. They were still trying very hard but with 2 extra players on the field, Boronia were able to kick goals.

The last quarter got even worse for our numbers with another player unable to play so now we were 3 players short for the last quarter. Boronia came home strong and it just hurt us too much having 3 less players on the field.

We ended up losing 62-38. The girls didn’t stop trying, which was very pleasing.

Goals. Liv Haun 2, Haylee 2, Ella 2
Best. Mathilda, Liv Haun, Ella, Marley, Milly, Saanvi
