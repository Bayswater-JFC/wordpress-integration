Round 1

Great games today to all the boys.

On a glorious Sunday morning in down town Croydon, the boys were pumped and ready to play.

Our defense was strong, following the basics of getting in front, arms up and applying pressure.

The midfield was amazing at getting the ball and boosting it to the forwards.

The forwards really had some fun kicking a few 'sausage rolls' and getting around each other to celebrate.

To think where the skills have accelerated from this time last year to now is absolutely amazing. The coaching team could not be prouder.

Thankyou to all who came down to watch, help out and support the mighty Baysy Under 9 family.
