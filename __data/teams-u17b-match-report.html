Round 1

Vs Blackburn

Our lads really had a dip especially after QTR time and got better as the match went on.

We will improve by training hard and practicing our running game, of which we saw a few glimpses of today.
Some great contributors today were Jack, Max Power, Joey, Jag, Nerius and Jayden W.
Let's all get to training on Tuesday and work on improving our game.
 
Lachie and Nic.

Round 4

Vs Fair Park.

Not a great way to go into a match with 5 players not available which left us without a bench.
After QTR time the lads got into gear and matched it with Fair Park for the remainder of the game.
Ultimately the lack of any interchange was the difference in being really competitive.
Commitment from the whole team is required week in week out to be available even if carrying a slight injury or feeling not 100 percent.
The advantage of well organised interchange is so important at this age group in Footy.
Better players were Kodi, Cooper, Joey, Power, Jaydog and Alex.
