Round 1

Sunday was our first game of the season and a new team. The boys and girls were super excited to play and we had a number of players playing their first ever game.
 
Our coaches noticed some really great passages of play, which will continue improving over the season.
 
Our focus for the first few games is on field unity and working on a new game style. We expect this to take a few training sessions and games.
 
Although the overall result didn't go our way, we didn't give up at any stage which is a credit to the team in their first competitive hit out. 
 
There are a few areas for improvement, which will be a focus over the next few training sessions.
